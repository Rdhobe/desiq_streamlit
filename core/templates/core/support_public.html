Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with DesiQ?

+

Getting started is easy! Simply create a free account, complete the personality assessment, and you'll be matched with decision-making mentors that fit your style. From there, you can start exploring scenarios or get help with your own decisions.

What types of decisions can DesiQ help with?

+

DesiQ can assist with a wide range of decisions, from everyday choices to major life decisions. This includes career moves, financial decisions, relationship choices, business strategies, and personal development plans. Our mentors are specialized in different domains to provide the best guidance.

Is my personal information secure?

+

Absolutely! We take data privacy very seriously. All your personal information and decision scenarios are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent. You can review our privacy policy for more details.

What's the difference between free and premium plans?

+

Free accounts give you access to basic mentors, community discussions, and a limited number of decision scenarios each month. Premium users enjoy unlimited scenarios, access to all specialized mentors, advanced analytics on their decision patterns, and priority support.
